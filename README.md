Step 1 — Landlord Setup (you see this when you first open the file)
Fill in: Property address, both party names, dates, panel limits, any restrictions or special notes. Click "Generate Tenant Link" → a shareable link appears. Send it to your tenant via WhatsApp, SMS, or email.
Step 2 — Tenant View (tenant opens their link)
All landlord-filled fields arrive pre-filled and greyed out / locked — tenant cannot change them. They fill only their part: installer name, install location, tick all 5 acknowledgements, sign, and click "Submit to Landlord". A Landlord Review Link is instantly generated — they copy and WhatsApp it to you.
Step 3 — Landlord Review (you open the review link)
You see the complete filled agreement — all tenant data is read-only. The only editable section is your Decision box at the bottom: approve or reject, add comments, countersign, print your name. Click "Approve & Generate Final Record" → a Final Record Link is generated to send back to the tenant as their copy.
